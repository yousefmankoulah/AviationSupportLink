Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I reset my progress?

    If you'd like to reset your progress, go to the settings menu within the app and tap on "Reset Progress". This will erase your current test data.

  • How do I access more practice questions?

    More practice questions can be unlocked by upgrading to the premium version. Visit the in-app store to view your options.

  • I'm having trouble logging in. What can I do?

    Ensure that you are connected to the internet and that you are using the correct username and password. If the issue persists, contact support.
